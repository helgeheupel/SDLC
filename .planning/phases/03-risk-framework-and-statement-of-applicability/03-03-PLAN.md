The workshop preparation document MUST contain:

   - Purpose: Validate pre-seeded risk register, finalize ratings, determine treatment strategies, accept residual risk
   - Date: [TO BE SCHEDULED]
   - Duration: 4 hours (with breaks)
   - Location: Remote (video conference)
   - Reference: Risk Assessment Methodology (HH-ISMS-RMA-001)

2. **Participant Roles**
   - CEO Helge Heupel -- Workshop driver, risk owner, final decision authority on all ratings and treatments
   - External CISO (NorthGRC) -- Contracted to Helge Heupel GmbH; challenge/validation role; provides second-opinion credibility for auditors; reviews and challenges CEO's ratings
   - Consultant (BearingPoint or EY) -- Domain expertise on regulatory, compliance, and industry-specific risk areas; CEO drives, consultant advises
   - CISO Agent -- Real-time capture; validates YAML schema compliance; commits decisions to Git during session

3. **Pre-Workshop Checklist** (3-5 days before)
   - [ ] Risk assessment methodology (HH-ISMS-RMA-001) shared with all participants
   - [ ] Pre-seeded risk register (36 YAML files in risks/) shared via Git repository read access
   - [ ] External CISO has reviewed pre-seeded risks and prepared challenge notes
   - [ ] Consultant has reviewed regulatory risks (RISK-REG-*) and prepared domain input
   - [ ] CEO has curated the pre-seeded list: flagged additions, removals, or rating disagreements
   - [ ] Video conference set up with screen sharing for real-time Git commits
   - [ ] All participants have confirmed attendance

4. **Workshop Agenda** (detailed 4-hour agenda from research Pattern 4)
   Include the 6-block structure with time allocations, activities, and expected outputs per block.

5. **During-Workshop Evidence Capture Template**
   Provide a template for capturing:
   - Attendance record (names, affiliations, roles, date, start/end time)
   - Per-risk decision log: "Risk ID | Pre-seeded Rating | Workshop Rating | Change Reason | Who Proposed Change"
   - Rating disagreements and resolution (critical for auditor evidence that the challenge role was substantive)
   - New risks identified during workshop (add to register)
   - Risks removed with justification

6. **Post-Workshop Actions**
   - CISO Agent generates risk treatment summary (HH-ISMS-RTP-001)
   - CEO reviews and signs risk treatment summary
   - Git PR created for formal residual risk acceptance record
   - Workshop evidence document committed to `risks/methodology/workshop-evidence.md`
   - Next quarterly review date set (3 months from workshop date)

Use the template structure from research Pattern 6:

1. **Document Control** -- YAML frontmatter with version, date, classification

2. **Risk Assessment Methodology Reference** -- Reference HH-ISMS-RMA-001; state methodology version used

3. **Workshop Summary** -- [TO BE COMPLETED post-workshop]
   - Date, duration, participants
   - Total risks assessed
   - Risks added/removed during workshop

4. **Risk Treatment Summary Table** -- [TO BE GENERATED post-workshop]
   Template table: Risk ID | Title | Category | Inherent Score | Treatment | Residual Score | Residual Level | Accepted By

5. **Risk Distribution Summary** -- [TO BE GENERATED post-workshop]
   - By category: count per category
   - By inherent risk level: Low / Medium / High / Critical counts
   - By residual risk level: Low / Medium / High / Critical counts
   - By treatment strategy: Avoid / Modify / Share / Retain counts

6. **High/Critical Residual Risk Detail** -- [TO BE COMPLETED post-workshop]
   "For each risk with residual level High or Critical, provide expanded justification for residual risk acceptance."

7. **Risk Acceptance Statement**

I have reviewed each risk entry, confirmed the treatment strategies, and accept the residual risk levels as documented. This acceptance is based on the risk assessment conducted in accordance with methodology HH-ISMS-RMA-001, validated through a workshop with external participants on [DATE]."

8. **Signature Block**
   ```
   Name: Helge Heupel
   Role: CEO / Risk Owner
   Date: [DATE]
   Git PR Reference: [PR-REFERENCE]
   ```

9. **Related Documents**
   - HH-ISMS-RMA-001 (Risk Assessment Methodology)
