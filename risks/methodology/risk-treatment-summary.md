# Risk Treatment Plan and Residual Risk Acceptance Summary

`[CEO-SIGNOFF-REQUIRED]`

## 1. Document Purpose

This document summarizes the risk treatment decisions made during the Helge Heupel Group risk assessment workshop and records the CEO's formal acceptance of residual risk. It serves as the auditor-facing package for:

- **ISO 27001 cl. 6.1.3(e)** -- Formulated risk treatment plan
- **ISO 27001 cl. 6.1.3(f)** -- Risk owner approval of the risk treatment plan and acceptance of residual risk
- **ISO 42001 cl. 6.1.2** -- AI risk assessment results including consequences to individuals and societies

This document operates alongside the Git PR workflow for residual risk acceptance. The Git PR provides the working process audit trail (timestamp, approver identity, file diffs); this document provides the formal consolidated summary for the auditor package.

## 2. Risk Assessment Methodology Reference

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Methodology Document** | HH-ISMS-RMA-001 -- Risk Assessment Methodology |
| **Methodology Version** | v1.0 |
| **Assessment Technique** | Qualitative risk assessment using 5x5 likelihood-impact matrix (IEC 31010:2019) |
| **Likelihood Scale** | Time-based, 5 levels: Rare (1) to Almost Certain (5) |
| **Impact Scale** | Business-survival-calibrated, 5 levels: Negligible (1) to Severe/Existential (5) |
| **Impact Dimensions** | Financial, Operational, Regulatory, Reputational; plus Individual/Society for AI risks |
| **Risk Levels** | Low (1-6), Medium (7-12), High (13-19), Critical (20-25) |
| **Treatment Strategies** | Avoid, Modify, Share, Retain (ISO 27001 cl. 6.1.3) |
| **Standards Alignment** | ISO 31000:2018, ISO 27001:2022 cl. 6.1.2/6.1.3, ISO 42001:2023 cl. 6.1.2 |

## 7. Risk Acceptance Statement

I, Helge Heupel, CEO of Helge Heupel GmbH, Helge Heupel B.V., and Helge Heupel Inc., hereby accept the residual risks documented in this risk treatment plan.

I have reviewed each risk entry, confirmed the treatment strategies, and accept the residual risk levels as documented. This acceptance is based on the risk assessment conducted in accordance with methodology HH-ISMS-RMA-001, validated through a workshop with external participants on [DATE].

Specifically, I confirm:

1. **All identified risks have been assessed** using the methodology defined in HH-ISMS-RMA-001 with consistent application of the 5x5 likelihood-impact matrix
2. **Treatment strategies have been selected** for each risk per ISO 27001 cl. 6.1.3, with "Modify" treatments mapped to specific Annex A controls
3. **Residual risk levels have been evaluated** after treatment, and no Critical residual risks remain
4. **AI risks have been assessed** with consideration of consequences to individuals and societies per ISO 42001 cl. 6.1.2(d)(1)
5. **External validation** was provided by the contracted CISO (NorthGRC) and consultant (BearingPoint/EY) who participated in the risk assessment workshop
6. **The risk register is maintained in Git** with full version history providing a complete audit trail of all assessment decisions

I accept the residual risks documented above and authorize the continuation of operations under these risk conditions until the next quarterly review.
